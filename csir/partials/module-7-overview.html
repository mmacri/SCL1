Module 7

Certification Exam

Objective exam aligned to CSIR objectives plus mini-scenarios.

Exam composition

  • 10–15 objective questions covering definitions, roles, scope, assumptions, classification, severity, reporting basics.
  • 2–3 scenario sections scored like mini Module 6 drills.

Passing and completion

  • Overall ≥80% with ≥70% on classification/reporting items.
  • Complete at least one full Module 6 scenario path with passing score.
  • On pass: display “CSIR Training Completed,” log date/score, unlock downloadable certificate.
