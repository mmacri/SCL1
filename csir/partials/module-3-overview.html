Module 3

Terminology, Classification, Severity

Core definitions

Cyber Event – unusual activity (e.g., benign scan).
Cyber Security Incident – malicious/suspicious act impacting a cyber asset.
Attempt to Compromise – suspicious activity with little/no immediate impact; still critical.
Reportable Cyber Security Incident – meets mandatory criteria and deadlines.
Event → Incident → AtC → Reportable Incident

Shows how terms overlap as impact and reportability rise.

Incident categories

  • Malicious Code
  • Intrusion Incident
  • Denial of Service
  • Reconnaissance
  • Unauthorized Access
  • Phishing
  • Other

Severity levels

Critical: cannot provide critical services; significant interruption to electrical power operations; inability to operate/monitor systems; utility‑wide data loss.
High: moderate interruption of critical services; business continuity at risk; data corruption; active malicious activity on critical systems.
Medium: loss of critical service to subset; potential impact to adequacy/reliability; detected precursor.
Low: minimal effect; services available with reduced efficiency; recon/suspicious activity; malware on non‑critical systems.
