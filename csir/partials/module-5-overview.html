Documentation & evidence

  • Record all incidents in the Cyber Security Incident Log (Tier 2 included).
  • Retention: final reports and data ≥7 years; logs/evidence ≥3 years or longest requirement.
  • ECS retains evidence and provides copies for NERC CIP audits.
Documentation checklist
  • Time of detection and classification
  • Affected systems and severity
  • Actions taken and by whom
  • Reporting timestamps
  • Lessons learned / follow-up items
