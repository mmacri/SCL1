Module 6

OT Scenarios & Drills

Hands-on drills replace generic decision tables.

Scenario layout

  1. Context: OT system diagram + story (e.g., suspicious logins on substation gateway).
  2. Identification: event vs incident, initial severity, notify who.
  3. Investigation: logs to review, systems to isolate, AtC/reportable decision.
  4. Response: containment options and ICS coordination.
  5. Recovery: move from incident to recovery, restore from backups.
  6. Follow-Up: documentation artifacts and lessons learned.

Scoring focus

  • Classification & severity accuracy mapped to CSIR tables.
  • Correct reporting and timing (1-hour initial, 7-day updates, 90-day follow-up).
  • Role-appropriate actions (All Staff report; Technical Lead refines and leads technical work).
  • Evidence completeness and documentation quality.

Simplified network diagrams highlight affected components, with a reporting “clock” that starts when reportability is determined.
