CSIR Overview

Purpose, goals, scope, and assumptions that keep the CSIR plan operational.

Purpose & goals

Increase security and resilience across OT networks and critical infrastructure, decreasing illicit cyber activity and improving incident response. Aligned to SCL’s cyber security master policy and NERC CIP standards (CIP‑007‑6, CIP‑008‑6, CIP‑009‑6).

Threats & failures

  • Malware or unauthorized access
  • OT outage and loss of monitoring
  • Credential misuse or tampering

CSIR outcomes

  • Resilient operations and safety
  • Coordinated incident response
  • Regulatory compliance

Scope & applicability

SCL OT CSIR
OT Systems
Seattle IT managed
Key scoping notes
  • In scope: BES cyber assets, ECAMS, PACS, and other SCL OT systems.
  • Out of scope: systems managed solely by Seattle IT for SCL.
  • Assumptions: trained stakeholders, current contacts, accurate asset inventories, recovery plans, backups, and spares maintained by system owners.
