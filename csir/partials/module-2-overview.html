Communications flow

Internal notifications move from local OT teams to Incident Commander and Technical Lead, with ECS and Compliance coordinating regulatory messaging. Severe or Tier 1/AtC cases escalate to City IMT and external agencies (E‑ISAC, CISA/NCCIC, regulators).

Role-specific prompt

Example (Dispatcher): “You see repeated failed logins on a control system. What is your next step?”

  • Recognize the signal as potential Incident/AtC.
  • Report immediately via CSIR channels (Service Desk/incident bridge).
  • Preserve observations (time, system) and await direction.
