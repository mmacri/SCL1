Help & Audit Etiquette

Need guidance on reporting, auditors, or escalation protocols? Use these quick references.

Audit etiquette essentials

  • Answer only what is asked. Keep it factual and brief.
  • If you do not know, say so and offer to verify and follow up.
  • Use documented procedures and evidence. Avoid opinions.
  • Do not invent timelines, causes, or remediation steps.
  • Ask for clarification when a question is ambiguous.
  • Confirm the exact artifact requested (log, ticket, report).

How to respond

  • State your role and what you personally observed.
  • Reference the CSIR plan, incident log, and approved runbooks.
  • Record the system time, time zone, and clock offsets.
  • Route off-scope questions to Incident Commander, ECS, or Compliance.
Always prioritize safe operations and evidence integrity.
