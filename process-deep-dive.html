Scope

What the process covers

The CSIR plan applies to OT cyber assets managed by Seattle City Light, including BES cyber assets, ECAMS, and PACS under SCL control. Systems solely operated by Seattle IT fall outside this scope.

  • Align incidents, communications, and documentation to the OT CSIR plan.
  • Follow the plan for incidents that impact or threaten OT reliability, safety, or compliance.
  • Escalate to enterprise responders when systems or ownership cross SCL boundaries.
High-level workflow

Six-phase process at a glance

Preparation → Identification → Investigation

  • Preparation: Keep plans current, confirm monitoring/logging, and stage backups/images.
  • Identification: Spot signals fast, classify, and notify the right leads.
  • Investigation: Confirm severity, assess scope, and align roles before acting.

Response → Recovery → Follow-up

  • Response: Contain, eradicate, and document actions with approvals.
  • Recovery: Restore safely, validate systems, and prevent recurrence.
  • Follow-up: Capture lessons learned, finalize reporting, and update the plan.
Move left to right. Each phase feeds the next and preserves auditability.
