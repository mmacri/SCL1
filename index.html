CSIR workflow spine

Preparation → Identification → Investigation → Response → Recovery → Follow-up

Each module aligns to the six phases. Start at the left and move right with a single, continuous runbook.

Preparation

Keep plans current, confirm monitoring/logging, and stage backups/images.

Identification

Spot signals fast, classify, and notify the right leads.

Investigation

Confirm severity, assess scope, and align roles before acting.

Response

Contain, eradicate, and document actions with approvals.

Recovery

Restore safely, validate systems, and prevent recurrence.

Follow-up

Capture lessons learned, finalize reporting, and update the plan.

You are guided step-by-step. Locked steps keep the workflow aligned and traceable.
